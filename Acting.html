ABHINAY

Behind the Scenes in collaboration with Ada welcomes you to Abhinay, the acting event at MATSURI, a festival celebrating films and filmmaking. The acting event is a submission based exhilarating event which gives artists an opportunity to showcase their acting skills, stage presence and creative insight.


Round 1: MOODS
 Record yourself saying "I love pineapple on pizza" OR "Haar kar jitne wala ko bazigar kahta hain"
Say that sentence in 6 different moods i.e Excited, Angry, Guilty, Shy, Frightened, Disgust)
The sentence should be said 4 times with each mood. No cuts are allowed. All the 6 moods should be performed in a single cut and should be there in one video itself.
The qualification to Round 2 would be based on the participant's performance in Round 1.
Round 1 Submission Date: 30th August 2021.
    RULES:
  • Number of rounds: 2 (both would be submission based)
  • The whole performance should be there in only one video.
  • Don't use any special effects. Use of foul language is forbidden .
  • Mode of submission: Google drive link.
  • Last date of submission: 7th September 2021
