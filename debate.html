The Cinema Roundtable

BTS x LoT presents MATSURI'S debate event- The Cinema Roundtable. The event will be built along the lines of a Group Discussion with total time being 10-15 minutes per table. To keep the focus of the event on filmcraft and cinema, the format has been formulated to minimise constraints and maximize fluidity in discourse.


Rules:
  • Each table will consist of 6-8 individuals, with a predetermined topic of discussion.
  • An individual can only speak without interruption for 2 minutes, at which point the moderator/host will request another speaker to take forth discussions.
  • After a 45 seconds block from participation, the aforementioned speaker will again be allowed to participate in proceedings.
  • The final decision rests with the Moderator/Host on all matters.
