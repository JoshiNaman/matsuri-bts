MATSURI, a flagship event by Behind The Scenes, and one of a kind Film Festival being held in Manipal for the first time. MATSURI aspires to provide a common platform for the world's cinemas, cinema enthusiasts and artists to showcase the best in film art; to contribute to the understanding and enjoyment of different film cultures in the context of their social and cultural ethos.

About Us

Behind The Scenes is one of the fastest-growing film-making clubs in Manipal. It has a record of making some of the best short-film, which have garnered praises for results in competitions like Mood Indigo or the Lift Off film festival.
